About Hacker Storytelling

I needed a way to evolve my storytelling in 2013. I was introduced to all the technology that make up Hacker Storytelling over the 2012 year.

These tools are meant to give me quick and simple ways to tell powerful stories that are support by open data and open APIs. As a programmer, I can build anything I desire, but simplicity, open and portability win.

I wanted a way to make sure every project saw the light of day from the beginning. I have too many ideas, that have been developed behind the firewall and because they don't get 100% finished. This new format requires I am open by default, and with very simple, client side building blocks it is easier for me to move projects along.

All projects (stories) I tell will start as Github repository, launch home page telling about the project and a blog to chronical the project.

Next I will be use JSON with Mustache Templates and presentations to support the complex sides of my projects and their surrounding stories.

My hope is to turn all my ideas, projects and stories into valuable content, stories, how-tos, data and presentations. As I do this I will make sure everything is licensed and accessible in an open way for re-use and contribution.

I will continue to add to this project, and encourage you to do the same. Let me know your ideas.
